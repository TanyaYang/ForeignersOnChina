Important Notice

Postponement of the Submission Deadline for “Foreigners on China” Video Competition


Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the organizing committee of the 2020 "Foreigners on China" International Short Video Competition announces the following decisions concerning the call for entries and related matters.

1. Based on the original six categories, another category "Joining the Global Fight against COVID-19" will be added. This category is open to participants around the world.

2. The submission deadline has been extended from April 18, 2020 to June 30, 2020 (00:00 Beijing time).

3. The award ceremony is to be held in China in September 2020.

Let’s help each other to overcome the difficulties. We are expecting meaningful and interesting stories from you!


Organizing Committee of the "Foreigners on China"

Important Notice

Follow-up Notice


In view of the COVID-19 pandemic, the organizing committee of the 2020 "Foreigners on China" International Short Video Competition announces the following decisions concerning the call for entries and related matters.

1. The submission deadline has been further extended to August 31, 2020, in anticipation of more stories about the fight against the COVID-19 pandemic around the world, as well as stories about poverty alleviation in China.

2. Award ceremony of the competition is scheduled in Yantai, eastern China’s Shandong Province, in November 2020, in which all winning teams will be invited to participate.

3. All the submitted videos will be released on Tencent Video, Bilibili, Xigua Video, Weibo and other platforms.

4. We will invest more resources and manpower in the dissemination and subsequent development of the winning entries and teams on various media platforms.

Thank you for your continued interest in the competition. We will do our best to spread your works and look forward to working with you to tell wonderful and interesting stories about China and the world!


Organizing Committee of the "Foreigners on China"

International Short Video Competition
